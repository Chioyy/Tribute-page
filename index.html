  • 1987 – FF1 for Nintendo Entertainment System

  • 1988 – FF2 for Family Computer

  • 1990 – FF3 Family Computer

  • 1991 – FF4 Super NES

  • 1992 – FF5 Super Famicom

  • 1994 – FF6 Super NES

  • 1997 – FF7 PlayStation

  • 1999 – FF8 PlayStation

  • 2000 – FF9 PlayStation 2

  • 2001 – FF10 PlayStation 2, Microsoft Windows personal computer

  • 2002 – FF11 PlayStation 2

  • 2006 – FF12 PlayStation 3, Xbox 360

  • 2009 – FF13 PlayStation 4, Xbox One

  • 2010 – FF14 Microsoft Windows

  • 2016 – FF15 PlayStation 4, Xbox One
